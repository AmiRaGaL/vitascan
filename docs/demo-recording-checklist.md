# VitaScan Demo Recording Checklist

Use this before recording a portfolio, README, GitHub, or demo-sharing walkthrough.

## Setup

- Browser size: use a desktop viewport around 1440 x 900 or 1440 x 1000.
- Use a dedicated demo account with synthetic data only.
- Do not enter real personal health data.
- Clear the browser console before recording.
- Verify API health before recording: `https://vitascan-jent.onrender.com/health`.
- Keep the recording to 3-5 minutes.

## Safety Language

- Mention that VitaScan is educational only.
- Do not claim diagnosis, treatment, clinical validation, FDA clearance, or HIPAA compliance.
- For red-flag scenarios, say the app surfaces emergency guidance and tells users to contact local emergency services.
